Claud bio

The claud is truly a majestic animal. Claud's most recent single "Wish You Were Gay" addresses the topic of falling for another person who does not share your sexual orientation.

Claud origin

Claud Mintz, known professionally as Claud, is an American bedroom pop singer-songwriter from the suburbs of Chicago.

Claud LGBTQ+ influence

Claud is leading the charge for LGBTQ+ representation in popular music. The singer, who was formally known as Toast before a lawsuit from Wonder Bread forced them to reconsider the name, identifies as non-binary and often addresses the topic in their songs.
